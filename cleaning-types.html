Chemical-Free

Steam Cleaning

Superheated vapor cleaning that sanitizes and cleans without chemicals, perfect for sensitive environments.

What We Clean:

  • ✓ Home floors and carpets
  • ✓ Hospital sterilization
  • ✓ Car interiors and engines
  • ✓ Hotel and restaurant sanitization
  • ✓ Kitchen deep cleaning
  • ✓ Curtains and upholstery

Benefits:

Eco-friendly (water only), kills bacteria effectively, reaches tight corners, saves time and effort, allergy-friendly.

Residue-Free

Dry Ice Blasting

Revolutionary cleaning using solid CO₂ pellets that remove contaminants without leaving any residue or secondary waste.

What We Clean:

  • ✓ Industrial machinery without disassembly
  • ✓ Engines and power units
  • ✓ Electrical panels and electronics
  • ✓ Food production lines
  • ✓ Plastic and rubber molds
  • ✓ Building façades and stone surfaces

Benefits:

No secondary waste, non-conductive, moisture-free, preserves surfaces, environmentally friendly, reduces teardown time.

Gentle & Safe

Soda Blasting

Gentle alkaline media that lifts contaminants without harming delicate materials, ideal for restoration and sensitive surfaces.

What We Clean:

  • ✓ Fire and smoke restoration
  • ✓ Aluminum and soft metals
  • ✓ Glass and fiberglass surfaces
  • ✓ Soft woods and delicate materials
  • ✓ Paint removal on sensitive parts
  • ✓ Automotive restoration

Benefits:

Surface-safe cleaning, minimal heat build-up, easy cleanup, gentle yet effective, non-toxic and biodegradable.
